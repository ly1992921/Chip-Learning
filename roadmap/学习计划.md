# Chip-Learning 6个月进阶计划 · 学习资源索引

## Month 1: 半导体物理基础

### Week 1-1: 固体物理与半导体基础

📖 **必读**：
1. [Chenming Hu] *Modern Semiconductor Devices for ICs* 第1章 — 胡正明（UC Berkeley）免费教材 — [官网](https://www.chu.berkeley.edu/modern-semiconductor-devices-for-integrated-circuits-chenming-calvin-hu-2010/)
2. [MIT OCW] 6.012 Microelectronic Devices and Circuits — [MIT OCW](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-012-microelectronic-devices-and-circuits-fall-2009/)

🎬 **课程**： [MIT 6.012](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-012-microelectronic-devices-and-circuits-fall-2009/) — MIT EECS核心课
🛠️ **工具**： [nanoHUB](https://nanohub.org/groups/u) — 交互式能带图模拟

### Week 1-2: PN结与二极管

📖 **必读**：
1. [Chenming Hu Ch4](https://www.chu.berkeley.edu/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Chenming-Hu_ch4-1.pdf) — PN结
2. [All About Circuits](https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/semiconductors/chpt-2/the-p-n-junction/) — 快速复习

### Week 1-3: MOS结构与CMOS基础

📖 **必读**：
1. [Chenming Hu Ch6](https://www.chu.berkeley.edu/modern-semiconductor-devices-for-integrated-circuits-chenming-calvin-hu-2010/) — MOSFET
2. **精读关联**：精读A04_DennardScaling, 精读A05_DarkSilicon
🎬 **课程**： [MIT 6.004](https://6004.mit.edu/) — Lecture 1-4
🛠️ **工具**： [Beta Viewer](https://computationstructures.org/)

### Week 1-4: 存储器基础

📖 **必读**：
1. [Hennessy & Patterson] *Computer Architecture* 第2章 — [Elsevier](https://www.elsevier.com/books/computer-architecture/hennessy/978-0-12-811905-1)
🎬 **课程**： [Berkeley CS152](https://www2.eecs.berkeley.edu/Courses/CS152/)
